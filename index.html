Hello! I'm Sarah Abbett

As a former flight attendant, I have seen so many beautiful places in the world. I've met so many interesting people. And, of course, enjoyed some amazing food!

  • I've toured the Palace of Versailles.
  • I've skied in the mountains of Niseko, Japan.
  • I've biked through the tulip fields of the Netherlands.

I've always enjoyed challenging myself whether it's running a marathon, teaching myself to sew, spending a semester abroad in high school or learning to make amazing sourdough bread.

I'm currently living in Eden Prairie, MN where I plan to start my next adventure as a software engineer.

My interest in coding began in middle school with a school competition, which I won. For years, I treated web development as a hobby. Then, as a member of a recreational ski club, I volunteered to update the club's website. Following that success, I decided it was time to learn and do more with my passion for coding.

I'm a software developer who is driven to learn. I'm excited to become part of a development team. As a software engineering student at Thinkful, I will receive the skills necessary to work as a full stack developer. The skills I'm learning include HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node, Postgres and React.
